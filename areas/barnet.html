Barnet FAQ

Do you cover the larger Barnet houses (Whetstone, Totteridge, Mill Hill)?

Yes — Barnet is geographically the largest London borough by area, and many of our jobs there are full 4-bed/5-bed family-home moves rather than central-London flats. We typically allow 7–9 hours with 3 movers and a Luton for a 4-bed Whetstone or Totteridge house. Standard hourly rates apply.

Are Friday afternoon / Saturday moves available in NW4 / NW11?

We respect Sabbath observance in the NW4 (Hendon) and NW11 (Golders Green / Hampstead Garden Suburb — technically Barnet) Jewish communities. We don't book Friday afternoon or Saturday moves there by default. Sunday is our busiest day in those postcodes. Other areas of Barnet (N3, EN5, NW7) we book Saturday normally.

How does the Brent Cross development affect NW4 moves?

Major construction at Brent Cross (station rebuild + Brent Cross Town residential development) affects NW4 and NW2 access on weekends. We check the council's road-closure bulletin before quoting any NW4 weekend move and route via the North Circular if the A41 is closed.
