Local intelligence

Things that come up on Camden jobs that are different from the rest of London.

  • CPZ + tradesperson permits

    The London Borough of Camden enforces parking aggressively. Our quote includes a tradesperson permit covering the whole visit; on Sundays and after 18:30 most CPZ bays are free, which sometimes pushes Camden Town move-day slots to a Sunday morning.

  • Hampstead, Belsize Park & Primrose Hill conservation

    Listed Building Consent rules cover façade and external joinery work in NW3. We keep handyman work to interiors, use reversible fixings on listed-building cornices, and route any external scaffolding requirements to a registered surveyor.

  • Camden Lock market access

    Friday–Sunday market crowds make lorry-and-van access into Inverness Street and Camden Lock impractical between 10:00 and 18:00. We book NW1 moves in those areas for early morning (07:00–09:00) or late evening if a weekend slot is essential.

  • UCL & Royal Vet term cycles

    End-of-tenancy demand spikes around UCL hand-back dates in June and September, plus the Royal Veterinary College Camden campus. Booking a fortnight ahead in those windows is the only way to guarantee an early-morning slot.

  • Mansion-block lift bookings

    Belsize Park, Hampstead and parts of NW8 boundary have mansion blocks with shared lifts that need to be booked through the head porter 24–48 hours in advance. We coordinate that as part of the booking — we just need the building's porter direct line.

  • Top-floor walk-ups in Kentish Town

    NW5 has many converted Victorian terraces with no lift and 4-floor walk-ups. We price moves at the standard rate (no walk-up surcharge) but plan for an extra 30–60 minutes per floor compared with a lifted building.

Camden FAQ

Do Hausio cleaners cover all of Camden?

Yes — every postcode in NW1, NW3 and NW5, plus the shared boundary areas (Bloomsbury, Swiss Cottage, Highgate, West Hampstead). Same-day availability is best in NW1 and NW5; in NW3 we recommend 24-hour notice because mansion-block access often needs porter notification.

Where do you park for jobs in Camden Town?

Most Camden streets are CPZ-controlled by the London Borough of Camden. Our quote includes a tradesperson permit (£12/half-day) for the visit. On Sundays and after 18:30 most bays are free, which sometimes pushes Camden Town move-days to a Sunday morning.

Can you handle moves out of Hampstead conservation-area properties?

Yes — we work in Hampstead, Belsize Park and Primrose Hill regularly. Floor protection for listed-building flooring, lift-up not drag-out of period staircases, and Listed Building Consent routed via partner surveyors when needed.

Do you do end-of-tenancy cleans for UCL student lets?

September and June are our busiest end-of-tenancy weeks in NW1 because of the UCL cycle. We follow the major Camden agents' inventory checklists and offer a free 72-hour re-clean if your check-out clerk flags an issue.

Can you fit a Pax wardrobe in a Kentish Town flat?

Yes — that's one of our most-booked Camden handyman jobs. NW5 Victorian terraces often have un-square walls and skirting that needs working around; we allow 2 hours for a full-height Pax build to handle that.
