Local intelligence

Things that come up on Hackney jobs that are different from the rest of London.

  • Hackney CPZ enforcement

    Hackney Council runs one of the strictest CPZ regimes in London — almost every residential street is covered. Tradesperson permits are bookable in advance via the council's portal. We include the permit in the quoted access charge and book bay suspensions for moves longer than half a day.

  • Hackney Wick freight elevators

    E9 warehouse conversions (Stour Road, Wallis Road, White Post Lane) almost all use shared freight elevators that require 48 hours' notice booked through the management company. We make those calls when you book and bring lift-wall protection on the day.

  • Bay suspensions for moves on London Fields streets

    Streets around London Fields (Mapledene Road, Lansdowne Drive, Richmond Road) often have no available loading bays. Hackney Council sells bay suspensions at £45/day with 5 working-day lead time — we book and pay for them, then add the cost to your invoice with the receipt.

  • Stoke Newington / Stamford Hill kosher requirements

    N16 has a large Orthodox Jewish community. We carry kosher-certified eco cleaning products on request for free, and our cleaners follow Sabbath-aware scheduling — no Friday-evening or Saturday bookings into known kosher households.

  • HMO end-of-tenancy bill splits

    Dalston and London Fields shared houses are usually HMOs with 4–6 tenants leaving on staggered dates. We split the end-of-tenancy bill across the tenants by individual share and provide one agent-spec receipt that can be used for everyone's deposit-protection claim.

  • Hackney Wick redevelopment access

    Active construction zones around Hackney Wick station and the East Bank cultural quarter regularly close roads. We check Hackney's road-closure feed before booking E9 / E20 moves and adjust the arrival window if a closure has been announced.

Hackney FAQ

Can you do removals out of Hackney Wick warehouse conversions?

Yes — E9 warehouses are one of our most regular East London removal jobs. We pre-book freight elevators (most need 48 hours' notice), bring lift wall protection, and price for narrow loading-bay access.

How do you handle parking in E8 and E9?

Hackney Council CPZ is one of the strictest in London. Our quote includes a tradesperson permit for the visit. For longer moves, we coordinate paid bay suspensions (£45/day, 5-day notice).

Do Hausio cleaners service Stoke Newington and Stamford Hill?

Yes — N16 has some of our most regular weekly rounds. Stoke Newington families typically book fortnightly; Stamford Hill larger family homes book full-day weekly visits. Kosher-certified eco products available free on request.

Are you available for end-of-tenancy in shared houses?

End-of-tenancy in HMOs is one of our highest-volume Hackney services. We can split the bill across multiple tenants and provide a single agent-spec receipt that works for everyone's deposit-protection claim.

Do you mount TVs in Hackney new-builds with metal-stud walls?

Yes — most E9 and E20 new-builds have plasterboard-on-metal-stud walls that need toggle anchors rather than wall plugs. We carry both and choose the right fixing on inspection at the start of the visit.
