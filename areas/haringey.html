Haringey FAQ

Do you check Tottenham Stadium matchdays for N17 moves?

Yes — Tottenham Hotspur Stadium matchdays close most parking around High Road and Park Lane 4 hours either side of kickoff. For N17 moves we check the fixture list before quoting weekend or evening jobs and slot accordingly. Midweek matchdays affect mostly evenings.

Can you handle Crouch End hill access?

Yes — we know the gradient and access challenges across Park Road, Crouch Hill, and Crouch End Broadway. For larger moves we use 2 Transit vans rather than one Luton, which gives us better turning circles on the steep narrow streets. No surcharge.

Do you do moves in Highgate Village?

Highgate Village is technically split between Haringey (N6) and Camden (N6) — we cover both halves. Conservation area applies, so we use reversible fixings and lift rather than drag period furniture. Listed-building work routes through specialist surveyors.
