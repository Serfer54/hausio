Do you handle moves into the Vauxhall riverside towers?

Yes — we work all the major Nine Elms / Vauxhall developments: Embassy Gardens, St George Wharf, One Nine Elms, Riverlight, Battersea Power Station (technically Wandsworth boundary). How do Brixton market days affect moves?

Brixton Market runs Tuesday–Sunday with Electric Avenue and Brixton Station Road pedestrianised 09:00–18:00. For SW9 jobs near the market we slot at 06:30–09:00 or after 18:00. For everywhere else in Lambeth, market days don't matter.

Do you do end-of-tenancy cleans for King's College medics?

Yes — Denmark Hill (King's medical campus) drives a lot of student lets in SW9, SE5 and SE22. June and August are peak end-of-tenancy weeks. We follow the standard Lambeth student-let agent checklists and offer a free 72-hour re-clean if anything's flagged.
