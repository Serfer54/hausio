Merton FAQ

Do you avoid Wimbledon Championships fortnight?

Yes — for SW19 (Wimbledon Park, Wimbledon Village) we don't book moves during Championships fortnight (last week of June + first week of July). The road closures and parking suspensions make moves impossible. SW20 Raynes Park is far enough from the All England Club to be unaffected.

Do you cover Mitcham (CR4)?

Yes — Mitcham, Pollards Hill and Tooting boundary are all within our standard Merton service area. CR4 has lighter CPZ enforcement than central London — usually free street parking, which keeps move-day total cost a bit lower.

Can you handle Wimbledon Village's narrow High Street?

Yes — Wimbledon Village High Street and the surrounding lanes (Lingfield Road, Marryat Road) are too narrow for full-size Luton vans during weekday business hours. We use 2 Transit-size vans + multiple trips, or we book a bigger Luton for early morning (07:00–09:00) before the village wakes up. No surcharge either way.
