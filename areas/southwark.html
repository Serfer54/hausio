Southwark FAQ

Do you do moves into the Southwark high-rises (One The Elephant, Strata, etc.)?

Yes — we move into the major Elephant & Castle and Bermondsey towers regularly (Strata SE1, One The Elephant, Trafalgar, Hawkins\Brown blocks). Each requires goods-lift booking 24h ahead and concierge sign-in; our team brings CSCS cards and ID. Move days are typically 09:00–17:00 because most blocks restrict moves outside business hours.

How do you handle Borough Market days for SE1 moves?

Borough Market runs Wednesday afternoon through Saturday — Stoney Street and Bedale Street are pedestrianised those days. We schedule SE1 jobs near the market either before 09:00 or after 18:00, or use Park Street as the load point if your address is on Bedale Street itself.

Do you do end-of-tenancy cleans for Goldsmiths students?

Goldsmiths is just over the border in Lewisham (New Cross), but a lot of student lets are in SE15 (Peckham), SE5 (Camberwell) and SE14 (New Cross Gate). We do a high volume of end-of-tenancy cleans there in June and September following the Goldsmiths agent inventory checklists, with a free 72-hour re-clean guarantee.
