FAQ

How much do home services cost in London?

Hausio London prices: cleaning from £22/hr (regular), £28/hr (deep), £32/hr (end-of-tenancy). Removals from £55/hr (1 man + van), £85/hr (2 men + van). Handyman £65 first hour, £50/hr after, with half-day at £215 and full-day at £395. All-inclusive of insurance, supplies and equipment. Bookings inside the Central London Congestion Zone (EC, WC, W1, SW1, SE1) carry a flat £18 access charge.

Do you cover all 32 London boroughs?

Yes — Hausio covers all 32 London boroughs and the City of London. Same-day availability is best in Zones 1–3 (central). For Zones 4–6 we recommend booking 24 hours ahead. Within the M25, pickup and drop-off are included in the hourly rate; outside the M25 we charge £1.20 per mile each way.

Are your team members vetted and insured?

Yes. Every Hausio team member is DBS-checked, interviewed in person, reference-verified, and we carry £2M public liability insurance plus £25,000 goods-in-transit cover on every job.

Can I get a same-day Hausio booking in London?

Same-day bookings made before 11:00 are usually fulfilled the same afternoon in Zones 1–3. A £15 same-day surcharge applies. For guaranteed availability, book at least 24 hours in advance.

Do you charge extra inside the Congestion Charge zone?

Bookings inside the Central London Congestion Zone (EC, WC, W1, SW1, SE1) carry a flat £18 access charge. This bundles the £15 daily Congestion Charge plus ULEZ for the day. Every Hausio van is Euro 6 ULEZ-compliant — no surprise non-compliance fines on your invoice.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Free cancellation up to noon the day before. Same-day cancellations carry a 50% fee to cover our team's time. Rescheduling is free at any notice — we always try to swap your slot rather than cancel.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No — we bring eco-friendly EU Ecolabel products and all equipment. If you'd prefer we use your own (for sensitivities or sustainability reasons), tick "I'll provide my own" when booking and we deduct £15 from the total.

How do I pay Hausio?

£0 at booking — you only pay once the job is complete and you're satisfied with the work. We bill the card you provide securely by Apple Pay, Google Pay or card after the visit. No upfront deposit, no cash required.
