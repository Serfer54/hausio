FAQ

Questions, answered.

Are your cleaners and tradespeople vetted?

Yes. Every Hausio team member is interviewed in person, DBS-checked, and has a minimum of one year's professional experience in their field.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies or tools?

No. We bring everything we need — cleaning products, vacuum and handyman tools. If you'd prefer we use your own products, just let us know when booking.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can reschedule or cancel for free up to noon the day before your booking. Same-day cancellations carry a 50% fee to cover our team's time.

Are you insured?

Yes — Hausio carries £2M public liability insurance on every job. Your home and belongings are fully protected.

Can I book multiple services at once?

Absolutely. Our checkout lets you add cleaning, removals and handyman to the same order. One team, one invoice, one contact.

How do I pay?

Securely by card after the job is completed, or by bank transfer for business accounts. No cash required.

Do you work weekends and evenings?

Yes — we operate seven days a week, 8am to 8pm. Sunday and bank holiday bookings carry a small surcharge.
