# Cold Email Templates — Letting Agent Outreach

Three variants by branch segment. Personalisation tokens in `{curly braces}` — fill in before sending.

**Sending strategy:**
- Send Tuesday–Thursday, 09:30 or 14:30 (highest open rates for property professionals)
- One email per branch per week — never two contacts in same branch in same week
- Reply within 4 hours when prospects respond — agents move on tenancy timelines, fast follow-up wins

> 1. **24/7 online booking** with same-day availability in Zones 1–3 (most cleaning panels are phone-only with 48-hour lead time)
