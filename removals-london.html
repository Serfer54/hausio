Removals FAQ

How much does a man with a van cost in London?

1 man + van starts at £55/hr with Hausio (2-hour minimum). 2 men + van £85/hr, 3 men + van £115/hr. A larger Luton van adds £15/hr. Bookings inside the Congestion Zone (EC, WC, W1, SW1, SE1) carry a flat £18 access charge.

How long does a 1-bed flat move take in London?

Usually 3–4 hours door-to-door with 1 man + van; 2–3 hours with 2 men. Stairs (no-lift Victorian buildings), parking distance and heavy furniture are the biggest variables.

Do you provide packing materials?

Wardrobe boxes, bubble wrap, mattress covers and TV/glass blankets are free. Standard boxes are £2.50 each, or bring your own. Pre-pack labelling kits are £8.

Are you insured for office removals?

Yes — £2M public liability and £25,000 goods-in-transit on every job, including office relocations. For high-value tech, fine art or antiques, declare the value at booking.

Can you do a same-day move?

Same-day single-item delivery and small studio/1-bed moves are usually possible in Zones 1–3 if booked before 11:00. Larger 2-bed and house moves need 24–48 hours' notice.

Do you cover pickup and drop-off outside London?

Anywhere within the M25 is included in the hourly rate. Beyond the M25 is £1.20/mile each way. We regularly do London ↔ Brighton, Oxford and Cambridge.
