## ⚠️ Honest expectation reset

Citations alone will **not** push you to Page 2 for top London queries like *"cleaning London"* or *"man and van London"* — those are dominated by GBP local pack + paid ads + DA-70+ marketplaces.

What citations **will** do:
1. Make you eligible for the **Google Local Pack** (3-pack) once GBP is verified — this is your fastest organic win.
2. Give you ~25–30 brand mentions across the UK web → strengthens the **brand-mention signal** that Ahrefs found correlates 3× more strongly with AI visibility than backlinks.
3. Lock in **NAP consistency** (one of Google's 5 local trust signals).
4. Surface in **long-tail London queries** (*"end of tenancy cleaning Hackney book online"*, *"man and van Camden hourly"*) within 4–8 weeks.
5. Feed Bing/Apple/data-aggregator indexes — which power **ChatGPT, Copilot, Apple Maps, Alexa** local recommendations.

**Realistic timeline to Page 2 (London long-tail queries):** 6–10 weeks if all of Phase 1 + 50 reviews + 3 service pages are done.
**To Page 2 (London head terms):** 4–6 months minimum, requires GBP + 100+ reviews + 10+ borough pages + paid digital PR.

## Review-generation engine (Phase 5, ongoing)

**The 18-day rule (Sterling Sky 2025):** if Google sees no new reviews for 3+ weeks, your local-pack ranking drops sharply. Once you have 10+ reviews you cross the "magic threshold" where they affect ranking (Sterling Sky).

### Targets for first 90 days
| Platform | Target | How |
|----------|--------|-----|
| Google (GBP) | 25 reviews, 4.7+ stars | Send review link via SMS within 2 hrs of job completion |
| Trustpilot | 15 reviews | Auto-invite via Trustpilot's free 100/month |
| Yell.com | 5 reviews | Manual ask |
| Bark / MyBuilder | 5 reviews each | Manual ask |

### Review request templates

**SMS (sent 2 hrs post-job):**
```
Hi {name}, this is Hausio — thank you for booking us today!
If we did a great job, would you mind leaving a quick Google review?
It takes 30 seconds: {review link}
— The Hausio team
```

**Email (sent 24 hrs post-job):**
```
Subject: How did we do, {name}?

Hi {name},

Thank you for choosing Hausio for your {service} this {weekday}. We hope everything went smoothly!

If we made your day easier, we'd be hugely grateful for a quick review on Google — it helps other Londoners find us:

→ {google review link}

If anything wasn't right, please reply to this email first — we'd rather fix it than read about it. Direct line: +44 7304 330614.

Warmly,
The Hausio team
```

> **Never gate** by asking "are you happy?" first — that violates Google's fake-engagement policy + UK ASA / FTC rules. Send the review link to **everyone**.
